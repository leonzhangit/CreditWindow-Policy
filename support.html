Subscriptions

CreditWindow offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions to unlock additional features. Subscriptions are handled securely through Apple In-App Purchases and can be managed in your Apple ID settings.

You can cancel a subscription at any time in your device settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Privacy

CreditWindow does not connect to banks, does not process payments, and does not collect personal financial data automatically. All information is entered manually by the user and stored locally on the device.

For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.
