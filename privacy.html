Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Credit Window (“the App”) handles your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

Information We Collect

Credit Window does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All credit card information you enter (such as card name, balance, credit limit, and statement day) is stored locally on your device only.

Financial Information

Any financial or credit-related data entered into the App is used solely for on-device calculations, such as estimating utilization, payment windows, and interest costs. This information is never shared, uploaded, or accessed by us.

Subscriptions

Credit Window offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions via Apple’s In-App Purchase system. Subscription management and payment processing are handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send you local notifications, such as payment reminders. Notifications are generated and scheduled locally on your device. You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Credit Window does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Retention

All data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Your Control

You are fully in control of your data. You can edit or delete any card information within the App at any time.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
